ABOUT

BeerWalks are guided city walks with stories and beer tasting - all outdoors.

There are beers, stories and walks in Copenhagen, on Bornholm and in the Faroe Islands.

It is always in collaboration with the breweries whose beer you need to taste and hear stories about.

Come with friends and meet new people, hear new stories, and try new beers.
